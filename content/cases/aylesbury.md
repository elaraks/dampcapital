The Aylesbury Estate in [Southwark](providers/southwark) contains 2,704 dwellings, spread over a number of different blocks and buildings, and was built between 1963 and 1977. There are approximately 7,500 residents. The estate is currently undergoing [a major regeneration programme](cause-effect-affect/regeneration).
